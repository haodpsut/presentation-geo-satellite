System Model & Formulation

Selecting the best clients by balancing three key metrics.

The Client Scoring Function (Φ)

Φᵢ = α·âᵢ + β·bᵢ - γ·dᵢ

α · Accuracy (âᵢ)

Prioritizes clients that contribute most to model performance.

β · Bandwidth (bᵢ)

Favors clients with faster connections to reduce transmission time.

γ · Delay (dᵢ)

Penalizes clients with high latency to minimize round-trip time.

The goal is to maximize the total score across selected clients while staying within a maximum total delay per round (Tₘₐₓ).
