Experimental Setup

Dataset

MNIST, partitioned across 10 clients with non-IID data distributions (500-2000 samples each).

GEO Network Model

Simulated central server with 10 clients. Communication latency sampled uniformly from [100, 500]ms.

FL Model

CNN with 2 convolutional and 2 fully connected layers, trained via FedAvg over 50 rounds.

Compared Strategies

SCA (Proposed)

Balances accuracy, bandwidth, and delay.

GREEDY

Selects clients with the highest accuracy only.

RANDOM

Selects clients randomly, ignoring all metrics.
