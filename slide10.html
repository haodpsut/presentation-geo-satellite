Conclusion & Future Work

Conclusion

  • The SCA-based client selection method effectively addresses high latency in GEO satellite networks.
  • It achieves high accuracy comparable to baselines while significantly reducing communication delay.
  • The method shows rapid convergence and robustness, making it suitable for real-time applications like disaster response.

Future Work
