Introduction: The Challenge

Federated Learning (FL)

  • Enables collaborative model training on decentralized data.
  • Preserves data privacy by keeping data local.
  • Reduces bandwidth needs by only sharing model updates.

The GEO Satellite Problem

  • High Latency: >500ms round-trip delays hinder frequent updates.
  • Limited Bandwidth: Constrains the size and frequency of model exchanges.
  • Slow Convergence: Delays and bandwidth issues can stall model training.

Research Question:

How can we intelligently select clients in a GEO network to minimize latency while maximizing model accuracy and convergence speed?
